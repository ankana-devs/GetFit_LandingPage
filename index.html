About GetFit

Welcome to GetFit — a premier fitness destination designed for those who demand results and refuse to settle.
Founded in Kolkata in 2015, GetFit has rapidly expanded to key locations across Howrah, Hooghly, North 24 Parganas, Siliguri, and Bardhaman, with further growth on the horizon.
At GetFit, we combine cutting-edge equipment, expert trainers, and personalized programming to deliver a truly elevated fitness experience. From high-performance training to holistic wellness, we empower our members to reach their peak — both physically and mentally.
This isn’t just another gym. It’s a space where goals are met, limits are broken, and standards are redefined.
